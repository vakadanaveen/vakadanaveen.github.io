I am a Doctoral Researcher in the HAIF (Human-Centric AI for Sustainable Future) program at the University of Turku, funded through the EU Horizon Europe Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA COFUND) initiative. I am affiliated with ELLIS Institute Finland and TurkuNLP, and co-supervised by Prof. Shaoxiong Ji (University of Turku) and Prof. Desmond Elliott (University of Copenhagen).

My research focuses on multimodal reasoning across vision, audio, and text — building systems that understand and reason over diverse modalities jointly. I am also exploring activation steering as a tool for interpretability and controlled generation in large language and multimodal models.

Before joining Turku, I was a Senior ML Researcher at Qualcomm R&D (2024–2026), leading multimodal LLM projects and filing 8 patents in audio and multimodal AI. I completed my MS by Research at IIT Madras (CGPA 9.38/10) and B.Tech. at VRSEC (CGPA 9.58/10, Gold Medal — Best Outgoing Student).

Chatbot system handling diverse audio queries via an intent classifier routing to expert models — ASR, audio understanding, and contextual reasoning components. Poster at EMNLP 2025 Industry Track, Suzhou, China.
